## Recording Tips

### What You'll Need:
1. **2-3 people** (one player, 1-2 actors)
2. **Good lighting** (natural light or ring light)
3. **Stable camera** (tripod or phone stand)
4. **Example words:** PIZZA, SUSHI, BURGER, MOVIE, DOG

### Camera Angles:
- **Over-the-shoulder shots** showing the phone screen
- **Close-ups** of phone tilting
- **Wide shots** showing the whole group playing
- **Split screen** for tilt demonstrations

### Key Moments to Capture:
1. ‚úÖ Phone on forehead with word visible to camera
